About us

Skiing

We have 5.5 square kilometers of skiing and snowboarding area, the largest in New Zealand.

Chalets

Our club include 3 chalets for you to stay in, which are all right next to the skiing and snowboarding mountains.

Membership

We have a very simple membership process. Members are able to book the chalets from Friday to Sunday.
Chalets
KAKAPO

The gargantuan of all the chalets, accomodating up to 30 children and 10 adults. It has a large dining room and living area, as well as hospitable services.

KERERU

Caters up to 21 people; strictly a maximum of 6 adults and 15 kids. Great for multiple families or groups wanting a Chalet for themselves.

PUKEKO

Caters up to 6 people, up to 4 kids and 2 parents. The best option for a single family. Contains 2 bedrooms, and a seperate living room.
